Frequently asked questions

01

What materials are the products made from?

For the manufacture of designer furniture, high-quality and expensive materials are used: an array of natural wood and a hypoallergenic coating. Also, close attention is paid to the modernity of the model and its convenience, that is, when purchasing such furniture, you can not even doubt its quality and durability. After all, the reputation of the designer depends on the quality and durability.

02

What is the production time for furniture?

Production time depends on the complexity of the design. Approximately the average production time for furniture is 2 weeks.

03

How is payment made?

Immediately after the conclusion of the contract for the provision of services for the manufacture of furniture, you make an advance payment (usually 50% of the total cost of the furniture). The final payment is made directly upon installation.

04

How is furniture ordered?

There are several options to start working with our firm:

  • You can call the numbers listed on the site.
  • Send a drawing with dimensions in electronic form to us by e-mail.
  • Visit our office.
  • After you are informed of the estimated cost, an official contract is signed and an advance payment is made (usually 50% of the total cost.). Upon the complete installation of all products, you pay the rest of the amount.
